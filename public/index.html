How to use Brightspace2Docx

Follow these steps to convert your D2L Brightspace course content into HTML or DOCX format:

  1. Export your course from D2L Brightspace:
    • Login to D2L Brightspace and navigate to the course you wish to export.
    • Click on "Course Admin" in the navbar, then click on "Import / Export / Copy Components".
    • Choose "Export as Brightspace Package" and click "Start".
    • Choose "Select All Components" then click "Continue".
    • Review the components to be exported, select 'Include course files in the export package' and click "Continue".
    • Click "Done" after the export is complete. You'll get a ZIP file to download, save it on your computer.
    • Please note: If your course contains a large number of embedded videos, the ZIP file may become too large for the conversion process. For best results, ensure your ZIP file is no larger than 50MB.
  2. Convert your course content with Brightspace2Docx:
    • Go to the Brightspace2Docx website.
    • Click on the Select FIle button and select the ZIP file you saved from Brightspace.
    • Select the output format you want for your document: HTML or DOCX.
    • Click the "Upload" button. You'll see a loading spinner while your file is being processed. Please be patient as this process can take a few minutes.
    • Once the process is complete, a "Download File" link will appear. Click on this link to download your converted document.
